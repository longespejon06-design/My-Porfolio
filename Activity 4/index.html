My Projects

San Lorenzo Ruiz Donor

The San Lorenzo Ruiz Diocesan Shrine Donor System is a specialized web-based application designed to modernize the way the shrine manages and records charitable contributions. In many religious organizations, tracking donations is often done manually through paper logs, which can be prone to errors or physical damage. This system solves that problem by providing a centralized digital platform that ensures all donor information is stored safely and is easily accessible to authorized personnel.

Arduino Robotics

Arduino robotics refers to the use of the Arduino open-source electronics platform to design, build, and program robots. It acts as an affordable, accessible "brain" for machines, allowing them to interact with the physical world through sensors, motors, and actuators.
